= Delete volume pairs
:icons: font
:imagesdir: ../media/

[.lead]
You can delete a volume pair if want to remove a pair association between two volumes.

. Select *Data Protection* > *Volume Pairs*.
. Click the Actions icon for the volume pair you want to delete.
. Click *Delete*.
. Confirm the message.
